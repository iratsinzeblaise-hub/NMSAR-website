About NMSAR

Nurses and Midwives Students' Association of Rwanda

Who We Are

NMSAR ni umuryango w'abanyeshuri ba Nursing na Midwifery mu Rwanda. Tuvuga ijwi ry'abanyeshuri, dushyira hamwe, dutoza ubuyobozi, dukorana n'imiryango itandukanye kandi dufasha abaturage.

Twashinzwe kugira ngo abanyeshuri b'ubuzima bazahindure ejo hazaza h'ubuzima mu Rwanda.

Our Vision

"To be the leading voice for nursing and midwifery students in Rwanda, promoting excellence and innovation."
